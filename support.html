Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a food or drink?

Tap the + button on the home screen, fill in the details, add a photo if you'd like, and save your entry.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Profile → Settings → Manage Subscription. You can also manage it through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings → Export Data to back up your journal entries.

How do I share my profile?

Go to your Profile tab and tap the share button to copy your public profile link.
